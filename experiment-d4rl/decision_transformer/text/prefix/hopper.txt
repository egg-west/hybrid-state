The hopper is a two-dimensional one-legged figure that consist of four main body parts - the torso at the top, the thigh in the middle, the leg in the bottom, and a single foot on which the entire body rests. The goal is to make hops that move in the forward (right) direction by applying torques on the three hinges connecting the four body parts. The action space includes Torque applied on the thigh rotor, the leg rotor and the foot rotor, all ranged in [-1, 1].
